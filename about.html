WE DON'T DO SMALL.
WE DON'T DO
BORING.

Born on London's most legendary food street in 2022, Fat Fellas brought something new to Drummond Street. While the neighbours have been perfecting their craft since the 1970s, we rolled up with a smoking flat-top, a head full of reckless ideas, and an obsession with the perfect smash burger.

Every patty is hand-pressed fresh. Every sauce is made in-house. Every order leaves the kitchen with something to prove. This isn't a "gourmet dining experience." This is the kind of meal you'll talk about on the walk back to the tube — the kind that gets sauce on your shirt and a grin on your face.

Bold flavours. Fresh ingredients. No nonsense.
